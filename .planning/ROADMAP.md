## Current Milestone

### v1.1 Rollout UX & Venue Operations

**Goal:** Make the live conference deployment comfortable on phones and on site by tightening participant document, QR, and 360 venue flows.

**Planned phases:**

#### Phase 8: Badge, Certificate & QR Check-In Hardening

- Replace fallback-looking participant materials with branded badge and certificate rendering
- Ensure badge QR points to a real frontend URL and produces a direct attendance result
- Keep preview and download flows reliable across supported browsers

#### Phase 9: 360 Venue Navigation

- Normalize named panorama scenes and participant-facing location selection
- Make hotspot labels readable and scene-to-scene transitions reliable
- Keep the participant venue page focused on route viewing instead of dense room tooling

#### Phase 10: Mobile Experience Compaction

- Reduce oversized blocks and secondary copy on phone screens
- Keep primary actions reachable quickly across landing, dashboard, documents, map, and dense authenticated surfaces
- Apply mobile-first simplification rather than only stacking desktop layouts vertically
